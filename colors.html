Recommended Colours

To ease the job of finding a good set of visually distinct colours, we highlight a set of (mostly) rather different colours:

The dark, deep and light variations of the primary and secondary colours: DarkRed, DeepRed, LightRed, DarkMagenta, DeepMagenta, LightMagenta, DarkBlue, DeepBlue, LightBlue, DarkCyan, DeepCyan, LightCyan, DarkGreen, DeepGreen, LightGreen, DarkYellow, DeepYellow, LightYellow.

Three shades each of the tertiary colours: Brown, Orange, Saffron, Flirt, Rose, HotPink, Indigo, Violet, Hyacinth, Cobalt, Azure, SkyBlue, Jade, SpringGreen, Chrysophrase, Avocado, Chartreuse, Pistachio,

The lightest six colours: Bisque, Magnolia, Periwinkle, Bubbles, Honeydew, Cream.

The most important shades of grey: DeepBlack (DeepBlack), Onyx, DarkGrey, Grey, LightGrey, Platinum, DeepWhite (DeepWhite); and greyish colours: Taupe, Lilac, CoolGrey, Lovat, Artichoke, Sage.

Finally, some colours which do not fall into these categories: Turquoise, Seafoam, Mint, Khaki, Olive, Bronze, Bole, Beige, Copper, Ochre, Goldenrod, Mustard, Apricot, Ecru, Tan, Coral, Vermilion, Mulberry, Plum, Ube, Orchid, Carnation.
